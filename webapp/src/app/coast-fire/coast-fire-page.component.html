Coast FIRE Backtesting

Estimate your Coast FIRE probability using real historic US stock market return paths instead of a single, constant return assumption.

How it works

For each horizon, the calculator runs simulations for every historic starting month and year, then applies year-over-year market returns from Robert Shiller's US stock market dataset.

A path counts as successful once your portfolio reaches your FIRE number within the selected time horizon. This reflects sequence-of-returns risk that constant-return calculators cannot capture.

Dataset source: Yale / Robert Shiller historical data
